Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Find quick answers about orders, returns, shipping speed, payments, and more. Expand a topic to see details.

How do I track my order? ⌄

Track orders anytime in the “Order History” section of the profile. A shipment email with a tracking link is sent once the package leaves the facility.

  • Tracking updates may take up to 24 hours after dispatch to appear on the carrier site.
  • If the tracking link is missing or broken, copy the tracking ID and check directly on the carrier’s website.

What is your return policy? ⌄

Most items are returnable within 30 days of delivery, unused and in original packaging. For full terms, see the Shipping & Returns page.

Read more on the Shipping & Returns page.

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
  • Some categories may have different windows or restocking fees.
Returns Refunds Policy
How long does shipping take? ⌄

Standard shipping typically arrives in 5–7 business days. Expedited options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

For region-specific timelines and carriers, see the Shipping & Returns page.

  • Peak seasons may add 1–2 business days.
  • Remote areas can require additional transit time.
Shipping Delivery Logistics
What payment methods are accepted? ⌄

Major credit/debit cards, UPI, and popular wallets are supported. EMI may be available on eligible cards during checkout. Also we are working on adding cash on delivery as a payment method.

  • For security, additional verification (3D Secure/OTP) may be required.
  • Prepaid and gift cards support depends on the issuing provider.
Payments UPI Cards
I forgot my password. What should I do? ⌄

Use the “Forgot Password” link on the sign-in page to receive a reset email. Follow the instructions to set a new password.

  • Check spam/junk folders if the email doesn’t appear within a few minutes.
  • For locked accounts, try again after 15 minutes or contact support.
