Shipping & Returns

Clear timelines and simple returns—find all shipping speeds, tracking details, and return steps below.

Shipping Policy

Orders are processed within 1–2 business days. A tracking email is sent when the parcel ships.

  • Standard 5–7 business days from dispatch; tracking may update within 24 hours of handoff.
  • Expedited 2–3 business days; carrier availability varies by region.
  • Processing Cutoff is 2 PM local time; weekend orders ship next business day.
  • Coverage Delivery timelines can extend for remote locations or peak season.
What if the tracking link doesn’t work?
Copy the tracking ID and check directly on the carrier’s site. If no scans appear after 48 hours, contact support for investigation.
Do you ship internationally?
International shipping may incur customs fees and longer transit times depending on the destination and carrier.

Return Policy

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery for most items, unused and in original packaging.

  • Eligibility Items must be new, unused, and include all accessories and tags.
  • Condition Certain categories may be non-returnable for hygiene or customization reasons.
  • Refunds Issued to the original payment method after quality check.
  • Exchanges Where available, exchanges can be requested during the return initiation.

To initiate a return, please contact our support team with the order number.

How do I start a return?
Reach out with the order ID and reason. The team will provide a return label or instructions based on the item and location.
Are return shipping fees covered?
Depending on the reason and category, return shipping may be deducted from refunds; policy details will be shared during initiation.

Helpful tips

  • Save the original packaging until the order arrives in good condition.
  • Include the order ID in all messages to speed up support.
  • During holidays, allow 1–2 extra business days for carrier scans and delivery.
